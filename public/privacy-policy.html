The App does not collect any personal information.

2. Anonymous Visitor Count

The App maintains a simple anonymous visitor count for informational purposes.

3. Local Storage

On supported platforms (such as web), the App may use local storage to prevent multiple counts from the same device on the same day.

  • Data never leaves the user’s device
  • Used only for anonymous visit counting

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase (Firestore) solely to store anonymous visitor counts.

Firebase is used without:
